# Background Videos

Place your nature/landscape background videos here.

## Requirements
- **Format**: MP4 recommended (also supports MOV, AVI, MKV, WEBM)
- **Orientation**: Any (will be auto-cropped to 9:16 vertical)
- **Duration**: 10-60 seconds works best
- **Content**: Nature scenes, landscapes, peaceful imagery

## Recommended Sources (Free)
- [Pexels Videos](https://www.pexels.com/videos/) - Free stock videos
- [Pixabay Videos](https://pixabay.com/videos/) - Free stock videos
- [Coverr](https://coverr.co/) - Beautiful free videos

## Tips
- Use calm, peaceful nature scenes
- Avoid videos with people or distracting elements
- Higher resolution is better (1080p or 4K)
- Name files descriptively: `nature_forest_01.mp4`, `ocean_waves_02.mp4`
